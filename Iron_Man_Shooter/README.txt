Iron Man Shooter - Full Modular Game
===================================

Run Instructions:
-----------------
1. Place your assets (images & sounds) into the "assets" folder.
   Required files:
   - player.png
   - enemy.png
   - bullet.png
   - Bg.jpg
   - Shoot.mp3
   - Explosion.mp3

2. Open your terminal inside the IronManShooter_Full folder.

3. Run:
   python main.py

Controls:
----------
- UP / DOWN: Move player
- SPACE: Shoot
- R: Restart after game over
